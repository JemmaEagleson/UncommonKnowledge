Logo

The Uncommon Knowledge logo must always be white in colour and placed on a solid background. It can also be used in the two variations as shown above. The logo is never shown in any other colour.

Usage

The Uncommon Knowledge logo must never be distorted or morphed and should stay a consistant size throughout. It should also never be rotated or flipped and must always stay the same angle. Words or speech bubbles (excluding the wordmark) must not be used around the logo and it should not be placed among other logos or pictures.
